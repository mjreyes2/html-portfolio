The Best Movies According to Marvin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Mist

This is my top favorite. It's a cool psychological horror movie.

Terminator: Dark Fate

This is a cool sci-fi movie that has several sequels. The first movie I saw was Terminator 2: Judgement Day. Later, I watched the original movie called The Terminator. After that one, I watched Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines, Terminator Salvation, and lastly Terminator Genisys.

1492: Conquest of Paradise

This is a historical drama movie. I watched this during my undergrad. It's about Christopher Columbus, and his interactions with the indigenous people.
